Animating and Modeling

Why modeling interests me?

I have started to learn modeling through Blender which is a free and open source 3D creation suite so that I may create high quality assets for future games. I also find modeling extremely interesting and complex.

Why animating interests me?

I am interested in animation because I find it cool to be able to create smooth and good looking animations that can be used in games or videos. I have started learning animation through Blender as well because it is free and offers many different tools that can assist me in creating complex animations.
